WellnessCheck keeps HR in sync with their teams' emotional health through anonymous daily pulse checks.
It provides a high-level view of morale, identifying trends and potential burnout before it impacts productivity.

**Table of contents**

.. contents::
   :local:

Usage
=====

*   **Daily Pulse**: Automated questionnaire at the first login of the day.
*   **HR Oversight**: Direct access to the "Management Dashboard" for collective visibility.
*   **Safety & Trust**: Responses are anonymous to encourage honest feedback.
